THE SOVEREIGN
NY CLOUD

A technical and political mandate for 2026-2030.

I. Executive Summary

NYC’s reliance on Google and Amazon constitutes a critical failure in municipal autonomy. We are currently funneling over $120M annually into a corporate ecosystem that utilizes NYC tax revenue to develop military AI (Project Nimbus) while subjecting city data to "egress ransoms."

The Mandate:

Establishment of the NYC Municipal Data Authority (MDA) to oversee a decentralized, city-owned, and union-operated infrastructure. We reclaim the servers, the keys, and the jobs.

II. Economic Multiplier

Unionized Labor Force

Creation of 450 new Civil Service roles. We don't just host data; we build a middle-class career path for local NYC engineers, Site Reliability Experts, and Architects.

The CUNY Pipeline

Mandating a 30% recruitment floor from CUNY computer science programs. We stop the "brain drain" to Silicon Valley and put NYC talent to work for NYC people.

III. Infrastructure

We reject the centralized "Mega-Center" model. NYC is a circuit board. We distribute the load across city-owned real estate.

Technical Scalability

Using Kubernetes on Bare Metal, we achieve "Google-scale" without proprietary software lock-in. As city data grows, we add Nodes (commodity x86_64 servers) to existing hubs. No subscription required.

IV. Data Security

01_Encryption Sovereignty

Physical keys held by the NYC Comptroller—not a private corporation. No FISA "backdoors."

02_Air-Gapped Systems

Critical life-safety systems (Water/Power/911) will operate on Municipal Dark Fiber, isolated from the public internet.

V. The "Nimbus" Exit

PHASE 1: THE AUDIT (Months 1-6)

Force disclosure of all Egress Fees. Identify low-risk data for pilot migration.

PHASE 2: THE PILOT (Months 7-18)

Retrofit Brooklyn Navy Yard. Migrate NYC Open Data (5PB). Cut Google invoice by 15%.

PHASE 3: FULL RECLAMATION (Months 19-48)

Full ingestion of NYPD, MTA, and Health data. Complete decommissioning of Google Service Accounts.
